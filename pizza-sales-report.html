Pizza Sales Report

This project analyzes the sales data of a fictional pizza restaurant to identify trends, peak sales periods, and customer preferences.

Project Objectives

  • Identify sales trends over different periods.
  • Analyze customer preferences based on toppings and sizes.
  • Provide actionable insights to increase sales.

Data Analysis Techniques

Utilized various data analysis techniques, including:

  • Descriptive Statistics
  • Data Visualization (using tools like Matplotlib and Seaborn)
  • Predictive Modeling

Results

Summarized findings and recommendations for improving sales strategies.
